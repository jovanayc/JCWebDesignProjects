Deposit Policy

Amber Nyree requires a deposit to be paid at time of booking in order to secure an appointment. This deposit is non-refundable, and will be kept if you cancel without rescheduling. If you have any questions about your deposit, please contact Amber Nyree.

No-Show / Late Cancellation Policy

If you cancel less than 24 hours prior or fail to show up at your appointment, your deposit will be kept and you will be charged the difference between your deposit and 50% of service price for cancelling with less than 24 hours notice and 100% if you fail to show up at your appointment.
